Patacones

Description

These are a delicious twice-fried snack that can be enjoyed by themselves, with refried beans, guacamole, or salsa. Or they can be served on the side with a variety of foods. Perfect for parties these are a simple dish to make and sure to please.

Ingredient

  • 4-5 large green plantains
  • cooking oil
  • salt

Steps

  1. Add about an inch or two of cooking oil to a large pot. Heat over medium-high heat.
  2. While oil is heating begin preparing the planatins.
  3. Begin by trimming the ends off the plantains, this helps with peeling off the skins.
  4. Next using your knife, make a slit through the skin along the whole length of the plantain. Careful not to cut too deep into the flesh.
  5. Now, remove the skin from the plaintain.
  6. Once the skin has been removed, cut the plantain in to 4-5 large chunks (or more if you would like thinner, cripsier patacones).
  7. Repeat steps 3-6 for the other plantains.
  8. Now that the oil is hot, place several pieces of plantain in the oil and cook for 4 minutes (or until they begin to turn golden brown) on top and bottom.
  9. After two opposite sides are golden brown, using the bottom of a place, smash the warm plantains flat.
  10. Return the flattened plantains to the hot oil and cook until desired crispiness.
  11. Remove from oil and let drain on a paper towel lined plate or cooling rack.
  12. Sprinkle salt on top while still warm.
  13. Repeat steps 8-12 for the other plantains.
  14. Enjoy!
